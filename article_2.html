Causes of lethargy in the body and how to get rid of it

Causes of lethargy in the body and how to get rid of it

Health

Mark Wilson

17 Dec 2024

Physical fatigue is a common experience that can affect anyone, regardless of age or lifestyle. It’s more than just feeling tired—it’s a state where your body struggles to perform even basic activities, signaling that something may be out of balance. Understanding the causes of fatigue is the first step toward finding solutions and regaining your energy.
Now, as 2024 comes to an end, it’s time to take stock. Which of our predictions came true, which developments exceeded expectations, and what did we fail to anticipate?

Lack of Sleep

Quality sleep is essential for the body to repair and recharge. Without enough rest, the body cannot function optimally, leading to feelings of exhaustion. Chronic sleep deprivation can also weaken the immune system and affect mental health.


Poor Diet

What we eat has a direct impact on our energy levels. Diets lacking essential nutrients, such as iron, protein, and vitamins, can cause fatigue. Overeating processed foods or consuming too much sugar can also lead to energy crashes.


Dehydration

The body needs water to perform most of its functions, including maintaining energy levels. Even mild dehydration can result in tiredness and reduced physical performance.


Stress and Mental Health

Chronic stress, anxiety, and depression can drain both mental and physical energy. The body remains in a constant state of alertness under stress, which can lead to fatigue over time.
